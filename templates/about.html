What is Neighborhood Help?

Neighborhood help is made for the sole purpose of bringing neighbors together through helping. Everyone has those tasks that would be so much easier with a little help.
On this site, neighbors in need can create a post that describes the type of work that needs to be done, the number of hours it will take to do it, and about how many people they need for the task.

Getting Started

If you are simply looking around, feel free to browse postings on the main page.
If you find a posting near you that you want to get involved with, simply click on the register button, create a new account, and now you can return to that post and send a message directly to the poster-in-need.

Interesting Tidbit

Even though we ask for your address when creating a post, it is not used for anything other that placing a marker on the map that is "approximately" at the address. A slight random modification is made to the Lat & Long coordinates to protect the privacy of the posters.
